What began as a passion became reality in 3 short months. In the summer of 2015, Daniel Huang founded Code Camp, a free summer camp to inspire students of all ages to code. Code Camp was focused on encouraging mistakes, collaboration, and critical thinking. While Code Camp is transitioning into Coder Central, its mission statement of inspiring our and future generations of students to think beyond what is presented to them remains in tact.
